Metal Working


In this course you will be learning about metal working.
Metalworking is the process of working with metals to create individual parts, assemblies, or large-scale structures. 
The term covers a wide range of work from large ships and bridges to precise engine parts and delicate jewelry.
It therefore includes a correspondingly wide range of skills, processes, and tools.
