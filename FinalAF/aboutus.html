About Us

We created this facility with one thing in mind. To help young adults as well as adults obtain the knowledge and information needed to grow and fine tune skills in fields that they enjoy. We strive to give everyone no matter the background, a stable foothoold in the field of their choice. To do such a grand task we cave come together with Techbridge, Tech Talent south, GE, Spotify, and a few others to create a place where growing, and eager minds can thrive in a hands on, in depth environment.


we provide numerous courses in different fields such as: metal working, Audio engineering, art and illistration, web development, coding languages, video game design.
